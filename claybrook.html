About

ClayBrook Zoo is a small zoo located in the heart of ClayBrook, Ontario. We have been in operation for over 50 years and are home to over 100 animals. We are dedicated to providing a fun and educational experience for visitors of all ages.

Our mission is to provide a safe and caring environment for our animals, while also providing a fun and educational experience for our visitors. We are committed to conservation and education, and strive to inspire our visitors to care for and protect the natural world.

ClayBrook Zoo is a non-profit organization, and we rely on the support of our visitors and donors to continue our important work. We are grateful for the support of our community, and are committed to providing a fun and educational experience for all of our visitors.

Thank you for visiting ClayBrook Zoo, and we hope to see you soon!
